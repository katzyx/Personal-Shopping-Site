How to Use: Suggested Usage:.-Carefully remove lashes from the tray, sweeping under the lash and using a lash applicator to lightly grip lash band. Gently lift the band away from the tray, never pulling on the lash hairs..-Hold the lash up to your eye to measure for sizing. If the lash is longer than your natural eye shape, trim the lash accordingly. .-Once your desired length is established, apply a thin layer of lash adhesive on the lash band and allow it to dry for 30 seconds..-Once the lash adhesive is tacky, carefully place the lash as close as possible to the lash line. .-Once comfortably applied to the lash line, finish with using a lash curler to help fuse your natural and false lash together.
